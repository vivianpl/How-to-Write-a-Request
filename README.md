# How to Write a Request. Linguistic and Visual Patterns in Greek Petitions from Roman Egypt

This repository contains the supplementary material and computational experiments for our presentation and published paper at the **31st International Congress of Papyrology**.

## Authors
* **Paraskevi Platanou** (platanou@aueb.gr) — *Athens University of Economics and Business and Archimedes, Athena Research Center, Greece*
* **Irene Chioni** (Irene.Chioni@UGent.be) — *Ghent University*
* **Klaas Bentein** (Klaas.Bentein@UGent.be) — *Ghent University*

## 📁 Repository Structure
* `List of Figures/`: Comprehensive list of figures, descriptions and permanent links to papyrological databases (`papyri.info`) and official image providers.
* `experimental_setup/`: Pre-trained Vision Transformer (ViT) models and pipeline instructions.
